TDEE (Katch-McArdle)

← uses lean mass
BMR — kcal / day (at rest)
TDEE (Maintenance) — kcal / day
BMR = 370 + (21.6 × lean_kg)
TDEE = BMR × activity_multiplier
lean_kg = lean_lbs ÷ 2.205
↓ TDEE feeds into macros and cut projection

4

Cut Projection

← uses TDEE
Weeks to Goal — weeks
Estimated End Date — calendar date
Daily Deficit — kcal below TDEE
Target Daily Intake — kcal / day to eat
Weeks = (current − target) ÷ rate
Daily deficit = rate × 3500 ÷ 7 (1 lb fat ≈ 3500 kcal)
Target intake = TDEE − daily_deficit
End date = today + weeks
↓ weight & activity feed water intake

← uses weight & activity
Ounces / Day — fl oz

Base = weight_lbs × 0.5 (oz/day)
Adjusted = base × activity_factor
Sedentary ×1.0 · Light ×1.1 · Moderate ×1.2 · Very Active ×1.35
